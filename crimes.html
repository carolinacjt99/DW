Editorial

Multimedia Object

"Crime Exemplares" - Max Aub

This project unfolds in an editorial and multimedia object that transports excerpts from Max Aub's Exemplary Crimes to the field of aesthetics and technology. Based on the themes addressed in the Spanish writer's texts, we sought, in design, to develop a concept that visually represented these ideas involving the viewer in a multisensory experience of apprehension of the past and connection to the present. Translated with www.DeepL.com/Translator (free version)

By Ana Catarina Lopes, Mariana Silva and Ricardo Sacadura 2020
